Selected Publications

Selected Publications

Unlocking New Possibilities in Ionic Thermoelectric Materials: A Machine Learning Perspective

Wu, Y., Song, D., An, M., ... Ma, W.* & Zhang, X. National Science Review, 12(1), nwae411, 2025.

This work built a machine-learning model for predicting the Seebeck coefficient of ionic thermoelectric materials and experimentally developed a waterborne polyurethane-iodide ionic gel through high-throughput screening.

Ionic Thermoelectric Effect within the Framework of Thermoelectricity

Wu, Y., Ma, W.* & Zhang, X. Physical Review Letters, 136(5), 056305, 2026.

This work established a thermoelectric framework for ionic thermoelectric effects and verified that the classical Kelvin relation also holds in ionic systems.

Self-Powered Tactile Perception Enabled by Thermo-Piezoionic Coupling

Wu, Y., Zhang, X. & Ma, W.* Nature Communications, In Revision, 2026.

This work reports a solid-state ionic gel with thermo-piezoionic coupling, achieving 93% accuracy in tactile recognition while addressing leakage and dehydration risks.

A New One-Dimensional Model of Thermoelectric Generators

Wu, Y., Ma, W. & Guo, Z. Y.* International Journal of Heat and Mass Transfer, 255, 127890, 2026.

Governing Equations of Thermoelectric Generators

Wu, Y., Ma, W. & Guo, Z. Y.* International Journal of Heat and Mass Transfer, 241, 126737, 2025.
